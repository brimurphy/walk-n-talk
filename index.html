Walk-n-Talk

Fun,friendliness and a good aul chat

About

It's about a community based initiative towards mindfulness and creating an awareness. Although these are strange times for us all, we can go beyond the external negative environment and seek fun,friendliness and a good aul chat on our Walk-N-Talk.
It was set up by myself Emma...a mum of twins boys who had an idea to try and create a community spirit in area's for people who like walking, talking and meeting new people. The idea behind it is for male and females (all welcome) to meet 2 - 3 times a week at one of our locations being led by a Walk-N-Talk leader and go for an hours walk. Bring your dog, friend or simply take your own path and come along. The times will be listed each Sunday evening on our instagram for the week ahead.

Locations

We currently have two Walk-N-Talk groups, one that meets in Dunnes Kilnamanagh, Tallaght and another group that meets at the Seafront Bray. Both groups meet every Tuesday and Thursday for an hour from 7.30pm until 8.30pm. On Sunday mornings we organise an early morning hike in different locations around Dublin and surronding areas at 9.30am.
