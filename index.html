About

It's about a community based initiative towards mindfulness and creating an awareness. Although these are strange times for us all, we can go beyond the external negative environment and seek fun,friendliness and a good aul chat on our Walk-N-Talk. It was set up by myself Emma...a mum of twins boys who had an idea to try and create a community spirit in area's for people who like walking, talking and meeting new people. The idea behind it is for male and females (all welcome) to meet 2 - 3 times a week at one of our locations being led by a walkNtalk leader and go for an hours walk. Bring your dog, friend or simply take your own path and come along. The times will be listed each Sunday evening on our instagram for the week ahead.
